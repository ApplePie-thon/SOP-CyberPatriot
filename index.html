Welcome to the CyberPatriot SOP Site

CyberPatriot is a national youth cyber defense competition where students learn and apply real-world cybersecurity skills to protect computer systems and networks.

What We Do

As the Cybersecurity group of the Computer Club, we train in Windows, Linux, and networking security, prepare for CyberPatriot competitions, and compete in Capture the Flag (CTF) events.
