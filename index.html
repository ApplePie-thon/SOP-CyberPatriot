Purpose

We streamline team creation, role assignments, training resources, and official procedures to ensure smooth competition preparation and execution.

How It Works

  • Sign Up: Students register for the Computer Club and choose their subgroup (e.g., Cybersecurity). Register here
  • Create Teams: Eligible students will form groups and assign team roles. When signed up, fill all the information in group page
  • Set Deadlines: Team creation and role assignments will close after a specific date.
  • Team Management: After creation, groups will have editable dashboards to manage info like NUC requirements, OS focus, and contact info.
  • Communications: Each team will have a private channel for updates and messaging leads or sponsors. This will be done through the software Discord. Find more information here

Resources & Support

Access a full range of training materials, presentation decks, and SOP documents via the resources page. Leads will maintain this content regularly.

Need help? Visit the help page to contact Mr. Miller directly via email or text.

Competitions

Check out the competition page for current round details, school rankings, and live scoreboard updates.

Important Notes

  • Archived data from previous years will remain accessible.
  • Discord is not the primary communication tool. Check this site regularly for updates.
