What is CyberPatriot?

CyberPatriot is a national youth cyber education program created by the Air & Space Forces Association (AFA) to inspire students toward careers in cybersecurity and other STEM fields. Learn more on the official CyberPatriot website.

Our Club's Mission

We give students hands-on experience in cybersecurity through real competitions and collaborative learning. Members train in systems like Windows, Linux, and Cisco networking while building teamwork and leadership skills.

How It Works

  • Students join the Computer Club and choose the Cybersecurity subgroup.
  • Teams are formed, and each member takes a role (e.g., Linux Lead, Windows Specialist, Networking).
  • Sign-ups, team creation, and resource access follow set deadlines.
  • Leads and sponsors manage registration and competition coordination.

Resources & Support

  • Training Resources curated by leads and admins.
  • All presentations and documents archived yearly.
  • Help Page to contact sponsors directly.
  • Scoreboards showing results after each round.

Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)

  • Step-by-step guides for Linux, Windows, and Cisco.
  • Checklists and competition prep materials.
  • Defined team roles and FAQs.

Our Goal

To create a professional, student-run system that prepares members for competition, fosters independence, and keeps communication and resources organized.
